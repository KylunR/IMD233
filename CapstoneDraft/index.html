Kylun Robbins Capstone Draft

I plan on creating a portfolio to showcase my projects, experience, and skills for my final Capstone project.

Who are you as a designer?

I am a functional and minimal designer. I believe the user's interactions with software should be seamless and natural. I do not believe the user should have any confusion when interacting with the system, nor should they waste any time having to deal with complicated or bloated user interfaces.

What is the most important thing you want people to take away from viewing your portfolio?

I want the viewer to take away key information about who I am as a person, a student, and a Software Engineer. This includes learning about my work experience, hobbies, projects, and technical skills. I would like this information to be presented in a clean and understandable fashion in which the user can access all the information they desire.

What are your three strongest skills?

I believe my three strongest skills are:
- Technical skills
- Organization of data
- Time management

However, I plan to greatly improve my design skills throughout the process of implementing my Capstone project.

What are you using for inspiration?
